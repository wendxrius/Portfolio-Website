About Me
Wendarius

Greetings, I am from the city of Prattville, AL as technology has revolved around me since a kid. I was always the "fix-it" person when the appliances around the house were not working correctly. This sparked my interest in this field as I was always curious about how certain devices behave and interact with one another. Then, in high school, I enrolled in a computer science class and instantly fell in love. My passion for electronics and computer programming came to life as I progressed through the course and experienced first-hand how important technology is to our society. Fast forward to my senior year in high school, I was conflicted between enrolling into Alabama or Auburn. I grew up in an Alabama football-loving family so that was, of course, my first option. However, as I began to do more research on the two respective colleges, I found how brilliant and innovative Auburn University is as they have received several nationwide recognitions for being a top-tier institution. So, I made my decision to become an Auburn engineer and chant "War Eagle !" along with my classmates in the electric field of Jordan-Hare Stadium. I never thought for a moment of regretting my decision and enrolling in another school. This place has given me a place to call home as the friends, connections, memories, and lessons I have learned are inexchangeable. So, as I draw closer to ending this chapter in my life, I am prepared to step forward into a new chapter and experience things I have never experienced before. I plan to continue this effort into supplying any customer reliable and quality projects. Thank you for tuning in to learn a little more about myself and my inspirations!
