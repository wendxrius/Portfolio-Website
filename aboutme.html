About Me

Greetings, I am from the city of Prattville, AL as technology has revolved around me since a kid. I was always the "fix-it" person when the applicances around the house were not working corectly. This sparked my interest in this field as I was always curious on how certain devices behave and interact with one another. Then, in high school, I enrolled into a computer science class and instantly fell in love. My passion for electronics and computer programming came to life as I progressed through the course and experienced first-hand how important technology is to our society. Fast forward to my senior year in high school, I was conflicting between enrolling into Alabama or Auburn. I grew up in a Alabama football-loving family so that was , of course, my first option. However, as I began to do more research on the two respective institutions, I found how brillant and innovative the college of Auburn is as they have recieved several nationwide recoginitions for being a top-tier institution. So, I made my decision to become a Auburn engineer and chant "War Eagle !" along with my fellow classmates in the electric field of Jordan-Hare Stadium. I never thought for a moment of regretting my decision and going to another school. This place has given me a place to call home as the friends, connections, memories, and the lessons I have learned are unexhangeable. So, as I draw closer to ending this chapter in my life, I am prepared to step forward into a new chapter and experience things I have never experienced before. Thank you for tuning in to learn a little more about myself and my inspirations!
